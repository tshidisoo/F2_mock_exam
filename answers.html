Section A

Grammar

50 points

Exercise 1 — Present Simple (10 pts)

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in brackets.

  1. He gets up (get up) at six o'clock.
  2. She goes (go) to school at eight o'clock.
  3. He doesn't like (not like) snakes.
  4. Does she work in a hospital? (do / does)
  5. She has (have) breakfast at seven o'clock.
  6. Where does he work? (work)
  7. My dad works (work) at night.
  8. He doesn't have (not have) dinner in the morning.
  9. She goes (go) to bed at nine o'clock.
  10. Does your mum start work at six o'clock? (start)

Exercise 2 — Present Continuous (10 pts)

Write the correct present continuous form of the verb in brackets.

  1. She is wearing (wear) a blue skirt.
  2. They are watching (watch) a video.
  3. He is eating (eat) the wedding cake.
  4. What are you doing? (do)   I am dancing with my friends. (dance)
  5. She isn't sleeping (not sleep). She is talking (talk) to her friends.
  6. Look at Grandma. What is she doing? (do)
  7. We are cooking (cook) and cleaning (clean) the flat together.
  8. The band is playing (play) music.
  9. They are choosing (choose) party dresses.
  10. I am taking (take) photos of the party.

Exercise 3 — Comparatives (10 pts)

Complete the sentences using: bigger than  ·  smaller than  ·  taller than  ·  shorter than

  1. The horse is bigger than the goat.
  2. The girl is taller than the boy.
  3. The boy is shorter than the girl.
  4. The sheep is smaller than the cow.
  5. An elephant is bigger than a cat.
  6. A baby sheep is smaller than a mummy sheep.
  7. The mummy cow is bigger than the baby cow.
  8. A goose is smaller than a horse.
  9. This cow is bigger than that cow.
  10. This goat is bigger than the other goats.

Exercise 4 — Adjectives (10 pts)

Circle all the adjectives in each sentence.

  1. She opens her yellow umbrella.
  2. He eats a hot pizza.
  3. I drink a cold milkshake.
  4. They've got long hair.
  5. He's wearing a big red coat.
  6. She's got curly brown hair.
  7. It's a sunny day.
  8. Put on your warm coat. It's cold outside.
  9. The little baby is happy.
  10. She's wearing a green and white dress.

Exercise 5 — Mixed Grammar (10 pts)

a) Have got (3 pts)

Complete with: 's got  /  've got  /  hasn't got  /  haven't got

  1. She 's got brown hair.
  2. He 's got a bicycle.
  3. I haven't got a sister. (negative)

b) Can / Can't (3 pts)

Write can or can't.

  1. She can ride a bike. ✓
  2. He can't swim. ✗
  3. I can play the guitar. ✓

c) This / That / These / Those (4 pts)

Circle the correct word.

  1. This These is my bag.
  2. That Those are my books.
  3. This That boy over there is tall.
  4. These Those are my cousins over there.
